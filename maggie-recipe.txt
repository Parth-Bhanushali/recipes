Ingredients:
 - 1 packet maggie (12 Rs/-)
 - 2 cups water
 - 1/3 tablespoon extra magic masala
 - 1 packet maggie masala

Directions:
  1. Take the medium size bowl and add water in it.
  2. Heat the oven for 20 seconds and add maggie to it.
  3. Wait 15 seconds and add all the masalas in it.
  4. Wait for it to bake till 1 minute.
